New in 1.1

Your battery, sous-vide.

Sous-vide holds food at one precise temperature — low and slow, never overcooked. Sous does the same for your charge: it caps at the limit you set so your battery never sits boiling at 100%. One password prompt installs a tiny helper, then it simmers away in the background.
